Yg dimana dia di awal selalu rendah diri dan pergi dari unia asalnya tp sama aja. Self-loathing is a feeling of worthlessness, inadequacy, and failure that can lead to depression and substance abuse. It can also cause feelings of inferiority and violence towards others. Symptoms Feeling like you're not good enough Feeling undeserving of love Feeling like bad things happen to you for a reason Having negative thoughts that are closely tied to excessive self-criticism Feeling like nothing you do is good enough Feeling unworthy or undeserving of good things in life Causes Self-loathing can be caused by a harsh inner voice that tells you that you're not good enough Self-loathing can be caused by a cycle of failure, pain, and ever deepening self-loathing Treatment Self-loathing can be treated by learning to break out of the cycle of self-criticism. Having feelings of self-doubt now and then is often not a cause of concern.
